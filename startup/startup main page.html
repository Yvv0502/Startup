Who are we?

ESSEC Consulting is a professional society founded in 2022 by Le Wagon and ESSEC BBA students. We operate on as a consulting firm, and aim to empower ESSEC students with professional practices and create value for our clients in a competative price. Our areas include market studies, communication plans, business plans, digital trasformation, and more. Our projects are carried out for companies of all sizes and in all sectors of activity.

We are a team of 30+ students selected from over 500+ students of ESSEC Global BBA. Most of us have accumulated professional experience working in organizations of various sizes from startups to multi-national companies all over the world, including China, Singapore, the U.K, France, and more.

We believe in the pioneer spirit of ESSEC, and strives to proide companies with best tool and most advanced practices to solve major problems and drive performance within the company.
