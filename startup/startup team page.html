Who are we?

ESSEC Consulting is a professional society founded in 2022 by Le Wagon and ESSEC BBA students. We operate on as a consulting firm, and aim to empower ESSEC students with professional practices and create value for our clients in a competative price. Our areas include market studies, communication plans, business plans, digital trasformation, and more. Our projects are carried out for companies of all sizes and in all sectors of activity.
